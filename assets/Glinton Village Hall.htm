WELCOME TO GLINTON VILLAGE HALL

A GREAT VENUE IN A GREAT LOCATION

Glinton Village Hall is the ideal venue for private parties, meetings, fund raising events, rehearsal space and various classes. It is very well used by everyone from pre-school children to senior citizens and is managed by the Glinton Community Association

NEWSFLASH

If you would you like to be part of the Glinton Community Association (GCA) to support and contribute to the valuable work it does for the community, there are 3 vacancies on the GCA committee. We are looking for a secretary and two more members to join us. Please contact us if you are interested.

There are 2 halls: the main hall and the small hall. They are divided by an acoustic wall which enables the halls to be used separately or, by folding back the wall, both halls can be used together.
